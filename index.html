Local Rules · Southern Illinois Masters
Rules of Golf
Official Local Rules · Tournament Card
1
Ready Golf. Play ready golf at all times to maintain pace.
2
Blue Tees. All players must play from the blue tees.
3
Ball Up / Bunkers. Play the ball up. Rake and place in bunkers.
4
No Gimmes. All putts must be holed out.
5
USGA Rules apply unless superseded by a local rule below.
6
Out of Bounds. Re-hit from the original position with a +1 penalty.
7
Hazards. Play it as it lies or take nearest relief with a +1 penalty.
8
Lost Ball. Drop in the fairway where estimated with a +2 penalty, or re-hit with +1.
9
Scorecard. Keep gross score on the official card. Handicaps apply after the round.
10
Tiebreaker. Ties are broken by scorecard playoff — lowest net score on Hole 1 wins. If still tied, proceed to Hole 2, and so on.
